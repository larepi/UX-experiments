Step 4 of 5
Add 2 steps to your CI pipeline
These two commands tell Smart Tests what was built and what test results came out.
Step 1 — Record your build
Add this before running tests:
# Add to your CI config — before test step
launchable record build \
  --name $BUILD_ID \
  --source .
Step 2 — Record test results
Add this after your test runner, pointing to your results:
# After your tests run
launchable record tests pytest \
  ./test-results/*.xml
📋 Supports pytest, Maven, Gradle, Jest, Cypress, RSpec, GoogleTest and more. Full runner list →
